Tags: LLMs, NLP, Fine-Tuning, LoRA, Model Optimization, Classification, MLOps
Evidence Type: Project

Context:
Developed an NLP pipeline to classify real-world job descriptions into structured categories by fine-tuning lightweight LLMs, addressing challenges of noisy and unstructured recruitment data.

What I Did:
Fine-tuned LLaMA-3 (1B and 3B) models using the Unsloth framework with LoRA and quantization to enable efficient training on limited compute. Cleaned and structured raw job description data into high-quality labeled datasets.
